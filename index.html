NextBuzz
Sept. 2016 - May. 2018
http://www.nextbuzz.gatech.edu/
In partnership with Georgia Tech Parking and Transportation Services, NextBuzz was implemented in 2013 to adjust vehicle layover time based on real-time data with the help of machine learning models. The system decreased vehicle headway and reduced wait times for campus transit, utilizing devices such as RFID scanners & Bluetooth iBeacons to collect data. (Android, Estimote, Firebase, iBeacons, Google Maps)
NeighborBonus
Oct. 2017
https://devpost.com/software/refer-4-in-com-tf9goj
NeighborBonus helps residents share their apartment bonus with future neighbors. Often times, referral bonuses go unclaimed merely because there is no acquaintance in that apartment; however, an apartment searcher may reach out to currennt tenants to hear about their first-hand experience living in that neighborhood, and in return share a portion of the referral bonus. This is a win-win-win scenario for current residents, future residents, and apartments because the first two parties receive a huge bonus compared to effort and time spent, and the apartment companies now have a more affordable marketing platform. (Angular 6, NodeJS, Typescript, MongoDB, Google Maps, AWS)
